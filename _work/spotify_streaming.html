Case Study

A hub of online concerts and experiences with features that create unique moments, bringing audience and artists closer.

The Problem

With the pandemic all concerts had to be cancelled. Streaming appeared as an alternative for artists to create more connection with the audience and also as an additional source of income.

Research

We analysed more than 20 websites to help us understand the market and the users, their needs and goals.
