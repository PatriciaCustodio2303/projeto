Case Study

The Challenge

The challenge was to create an online store for a physical product using a Design system for both desktop and mobile versions.

I chose to design an online store for a brand of coffee beans called “Brew Pods”.

Work Process

Concept

Brew Pods is a coffee beans online shop where people can buy 3 different types of coffee beans individually or by subscription.

The interface was designed according to the values of the brand: a classic but modern store with a high-quality product.
